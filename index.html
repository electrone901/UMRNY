About Us

United Mexican Runners of New York (UMRNY) is an organization to promote the values of sport, leadership and education among the Mexican and Latino community in New York and surrounding areas. The organization includes a running team registered as a local club in the New York Road Runners circuit of races. Want to support our programs?

Membership Run with us

There are several resources in the organization to help its members prepare well for events and races. Experienced coaches and a warm and welcome community where you can train in groups in different locations in New York. The team usually meets for training on Tuesday morning and Friday afternoon at Astoria Park and Sunday morning at Central Park If you are interested in joining our community please contact us at: unitedmexicanrunnersofny@gmail.com

Training Programs

The team's main focus for the remainder of the year are the Chicago and the New Marathons. The training programs include a mix of speed sessions and long runs. We meet regularly on Fridays at 6pm at Astoria Park to do speed drills and Sunday morning in Central Park to do long runs. Please find current training programs below:
